ZetiRover

Portable repeater for commercial vehicles and machinery. The ZetiRover takes your patchy 3G/4G signal and punches out a high bandwidth Wi-Fi network up to 300 metres around your vehicle. Multi-carrier, multi-sim, multi-antenna, 3G, 4G and Wi-Fi for best in class connectivity. Be connected wherever you roam.

Coverage

High speed connectivity on and off farm for commercial vehicles. Made for Wi-Fi calling and internet access where and when you need it.

Multi-Carrier

Seamless handover between multi-carrier 3G, 4G and Wi-Fi networks means you can be connected, productive and safe in more places than ever before.

Magnetised Quick Mount

Magnetic quick mount to install in seconds. Easily moved between vehicles and machinery.

Fill those blackspots

The ZetiRover was designed with on farm connectivity in mind from the start. Boosters and repeaters are suitable for improving coverage for voice in areas where there is unreliable signal strength. However the simple fact is that many Australian farms don’t have enough coverage to boost in the first place. Most farms have many black spots where a booster or repeater simply cannot get enough signal to work reliably. ZetiRover can work with a private Wi-Fi network to fill in mobile blackspots anywhere you need coverage using Zetifi’s range of Farm Wide Wi-Fi products.

Designed to work with any vehicle. Anywhere!

The ZetiRover is compatible with any of your vehicles or machinery. Simply mount on top.

Vehicles
