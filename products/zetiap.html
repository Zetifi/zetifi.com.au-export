ZetiAP

The ZetiAP is a ruggedised wireless access point suitable for extending Wi-Fi connectivity into sheds, yards and houses.

Coverage

Wi-Fi calling in mobile blackspots and internet access for when are where you need it with 200m line of sight range to phones, tablets, computers and more.

Shared Connectivity Simplified

With on board power passthrough and remote config support you can install a ZetiAP in the house (or in the roof) and run a single cable to a wireless bridge on the roof to create point to point links spanning 10km or more.

Made to Last

It’s heat tolerant (up to 70 degrees C) so is tough enough to be installed inside roof spaces and it’s weatherproof & dustproof so can be installed inside or outside.
