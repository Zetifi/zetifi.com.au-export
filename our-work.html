Our Work

Our journey so far.

2018

- R&D projects with NT DPI. Off-grid WiFi projects successfully completed at Douglas Daly Research Farm and Victoria River Research Station

- Invention of and provisional patent application for ‘Sleepy’ Networks.

2019

- Jobs for NSW MVP Grant project completed demonstrating ‘Sleepy’ Networks provides real value in building solar powered WiFi networks.

- SparkLabs Cultiv8 Accelerator through the GATE at NSW DPI Orange

- muru-D (Telstra) Accelerator program

- Capital raise from investors including SparkLabs and others.

- Team grows from 1 to 3

- Pilot agreement with Case IH to trial sale and installation of Zetifi products.

-Accelerating Commercialisation grant to launch ‘Sleepy’ WiFi Repeater

- 50 prototype systems deployed

2020

- Capital raise from investors including SparkLabs, GRDC GrainInnov8 and CEFC Clean Energy Seed Fund and others.

- Team grows from 3 to 10

- Trial of Zetifi product prototypes completed through WA DPIRD Decision IoT projects.

- Trial of Zetifi prototypes completed through NSW DPI Regional Digital Connectivity projects.

- Trials of ZetiCell and other Zetifi prototypes by Birchip Cropping Group

- 100+ systems deployed
